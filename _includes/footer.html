Hi my name is Pamela, I'm a designer and now developer who's been working in the space of digital design and branding for over 10 years.

I'm a bit of an alien but I have an open mind and I love a good challenge. My design practice and experience spans across multiple disciplines because I enjoy the flexibility of being able to do all the things and evolving alongside new technologies, most recently in the areas of full-stack development and 3D. I've spent the last 4 years freelancing and starting DIY non-for-profit projects within my local arts, music and film community in Meanjin Brisbane. Fostering underground culture and community is at the centre of what I love to do. I am also a ceramic artist making one-of-a-kind lamps under the moniker Queena Grot and play in a punk band called Guppy.
